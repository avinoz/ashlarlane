Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any additional or hidden fees?

No setup charges. State-mandated fees are passed through at cost with no markup.

Is there a minimum commitment?

No. You can transfer your license out anytime.

Do you accept new licensees?

Yes. New agents receive transaction mentorship on early closings. We teach contracts and compliance, not lead generation.

What do you expect and allow?

No mandatory meetings. Work with buyers and sellers, use your own brand, sell your own rehabs, and assign off-market contracts within local rules and firm policies.

Do you offer discounts for teams?

Yes. On the upgraded plan, add agents for $50/mo each. If a team splits, agents keep the discounted rate for six months.

How do I transfer my license?

Call us at (415) 555-0100 and we will walk you through the steps. Most transfers take just a few minutes.

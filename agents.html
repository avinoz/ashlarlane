Frequently Asked Questions

What is the total charge per closed transaction?

Ashlar Lane uses a simple model: a monthly plan fee plus a flat closing fee per transaction — not a percentage of your commission. Plans start at $85/month + $550 per closing; our Business plan is $125/month + $450 per closing. See commission plans for full details. State-mandated fees are passed through at cost with no markup.

How soon do I get paid after escrow closes?

Once your transaction file is approved and we receive confirmed closing documents, commission disbursements are processed promptly — typically within one to two business days.

Can I negotiate my commission with clients?

Yes. You set your own rates with buyers and sellers. The only requirement is that your commission covers Ashlar Lane's closing fee for your plan.

How do I get broker support?

Reach our managing brokers by phone, email, or text during business hours at (415) 555-0100. For urgent transaction questions that cannot wait until the next business day, after-hours contact information is provided in your agent welcome materials.

Where do I get C.A.R. and transaction forms?

California agents typically access C.A.R. forms through ZipForm at car.org, included with your local association membership. If you need help setting up access, our team can walk you through the process.

What about REALTOR® memberships and MLS access?

Ashlar Lane is a California-licensed brokerage; each agent maintains their own REALTOR® association and MLS membership for the California markets they serve. Dues and requirements vary by local board — we help you navigate Bay Area and statewide requirements.

How do I know what is required for my transaction?

When you open a transaction in our system, you will see a checklist of required documents based on the deal type — listing, purchase, referral, and so on. If anything is unclear, your transaction coordinator or managing broker can help.

Can I join if I also hold an NMLS mortgage license?

Often, yes. Agents who hold a separate NMLS license under another mortgage broker can typically keep their real estate license with Ashlar Lane, provided your real estate transactions flow through our brokerage and the two businesses are marketed separately. Contact us before joining to confirm your specific situation.

Can my clients use any lender, title company, or escrow provider?

Yes. Your clients may work with any lender, title company, or escrow provider they choose. We can recommend trusted Bay Area vendors when helpful, but the decision is yours and your client's.

Do you accept part-time agents?

Yes. We welcome part-time and dual-career agents. There is no requirement to practice full time to hang your license with Ashlar Lane.

Where does Ashlar Lane operate?

California only. Our managing brokers and leadership are Bay Area–based, and we support agents working across Northern California — from San Francisco and the Peninsula to the East Bay, South Bay, and North Bay. There is no requirement to work from a physical office; you run your business locally, with brokers who know these markets inside and out.

Can I use a local vendor for business cards, signs, and marketing?

Yes. You may order business cards, signs, and print materials from any vendor, as long as they follow our marketing guidelines — including the Ashlar Lane logo where required and your DRE license number. Marketing beyond basic contact information may need broker approval.

Do I need to join a local association to do business?

Yes. To list and sell property, you will need active membership with a local REALTOR® association that includes MLS access for your market. That membership is between you and the association, not the brokerage — but we will guide you through the steps when you join.

Do you offer lead generation training?

We provide resources to help you build your business, including guidance on lead generation and client follow-up. Our core focus is transaction support, compliance, and responsive broker access — not running a lead-generation program for you.

Do you provide leads?

Ashlar Lane does not guarantee leads. Many of our agents generate their own business or use third-party lead services on their own. We focus on helping you close the deals you bring in with strong broker support and transaction coordination.

Do I receive anything if I refer agents to join?

Yes. If you refer licensed agents who join Ashlar Lane and close transactions, you may qualify for recruiting bonuses under our agent referral program. Ask us for current program details when you join.

Is there a minimum commitment?

No. You can transfer your license out at any time. New agents receive transaction mentorship on their first closings — we focus on contracts and compliance, not mandatory meetings or lead quotas.

Do you offer discounts for teams?

Yes. On the Business plan, add agents for $50/month each. If a team splits, agents keep the discounted rate for six months.

How do I transfer my license to Ashlar Lane?

Call us at (415) 555-0100 or submit the form above and we will walk you through the transfer. Most California transfers take just a few minutes once you are ready.

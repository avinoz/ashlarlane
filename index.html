Frequently Asked Questions

Do I pay Ashlar Lane directly?

Typically, brokerage compensation is handled through the transaction according to local custom and your agreement with your agent. Your agent can explain how fees work before you get started.

Can you help with investment properties?

Yes. Many Ashlar Lane agents work with buyers and sellers evaluating rentals, flips, and other investment opportunities.

How quickly will someone respond?

We aim for same-day responses on new inquiries. If you are under contract, your agent and our broker support team prioritize time-sensitive questions.

What areas do you serve?

Our agents operate in multiple markets. Share your city or region in the contact form and we will connect you with the right local agent.

I am a licensed agent. Is this page for me?

If you want to hang your license with Ashlar Lane, visit our agents page for commission plans and brokerage details.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right agent?

Start by browsing our agent directory. Each profile describes their markets and strengths. If you are unsure, use the contact form and we will recommend someone on our Bay Area team.

What areas do you serve?

We operate in California only, with a deep focus on the Bay Area — including San Francisco, the East Bay, Peninsula, South Bay, and North Bay. Our agents know these markets inside and out; ask yours about specific neighborhoods they cover.

Do I pay Ashlar Lane directly?

Typically, brokerage compensation is handled through the transaction according to local custom and your agreement with your agent. Your agent can explain how fees work before you get started.

How quickly will someone respond?

We aim for same-day responses on new inquiries. You can also contact an agent directly from their profile page.

I am a licensed agent. Is this page for me?

If you want to hang your license with Ashlar Lane, visit our agents page for commission plans and brokerage details.
